From the Trilogy

From Darkness to Light

A passacaglia in F minor—twelve variations on an unchanging bass line. The same notes cycle and spiral, repetition slowly becoming revelation. What begins as constraint transforms into liberation, showing how we emerge not through sudden breakthrough but patient ascent, each turn bringing more light.

Theme & Variations

Echoes in My Chamber

A theme and fourteen variations that began with a simple melody—the kind that feels like it's always existed. Each variation becomes a conversation with musical memory: flowing Alberti bass, romantic whispers, virtuosic bursts, impressionist colors. The "chamber" is both the room of composition and the space where musical echoes live and breathe together.
